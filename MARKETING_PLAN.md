## Goals
- Drive kindergarten/daycare adoption through free trial invitations
- Use blog/video touchpoints to move educators and parents toward the live site
- Align the landing page, short demo, and outreach messaging into one funnel

## Key Messaging
- "Five-minute, install-free focus play; launch with a tap"
- Position activities as attention/memory/observation practice rather than medical claims
- Spell out teacher logistics (materials, timing, expected outcomes) on a single overview page

## Channel Activities
1. **Teacher landing page**: age range, session flow, benefits, free trial/contact CTA, QR/link
2. **Naver blog**: why to use the games, classroom stories, short guides, embed demo video/QR to live site
3. **30-60 second demo video**: show child response, interface, how to start in class; publish on blog/SNS
4. **Direct outreach**: list 30 local institutes, offer one-week trial, send teacher one-pager and request feedback
5. **Community posts**: after collecting testimonials, share concise summaries in teacher groups/mom cafes

## Eight-week Timeline
1. Week 1: build teacher landing essentials, add inquiry form/QR, film demo clip
2. Week 2: publish three blog articles, create five SNS shorts
3. Weeks 3-4: send free-trial outreach to institutes and distribute teacher guide
4. Week 5: turn early trial feedback into blog/SNS stories
5. Weeks 6-7: offer monthly or institution licenses and expand adoption
6. Week 8: double down on top-performing channels and refine messaging

## Extras
- Capture gameplay stills and provide a PDF guide showing exactly how to use the activity in class
- Link Naver TalkTalk, QR, and reservation tools to keep the exploration-to-trial path short
- Follow each trial with a five-question feedback form; reuse responses as social proof and product insight
